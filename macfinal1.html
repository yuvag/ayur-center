What We Know


Ayurveda : a way of life

Ayurveda in intricate holistic healing system of India, trace its origins to about 5000 years ago. Derived from the Sanskrit ayur(life) and veda(knowledge). Ayurveda is essentially a science of life based on the laws of nature. It deals elaborately with measures for healthful living during the entire span of life and its various phases. Besides, dealing with principles for maintenance of health, it has also developed a wide range of therapeutic measures to combat illness.
